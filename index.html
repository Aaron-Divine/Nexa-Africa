ABOUT

Built by Africans,
for Africa.

Nexa Africa was founded on one observation: African banking was never designed for most Africans. It was designed for people with formal IDs, formal jobs, formal addresses, and time to visit branches.

We built Nexa for the suya seller, the teacher, the market woman, the farmer, the student, the artisan, the small business owner. The people who hold Africa's economy together every single day — and deserve a bank that fights for them.

Nexa Africa combines banking, artificial intelligence, data science, financial education, and community infrastructure into one platform. It is not a fintech startup with a banking licence. It is a financial revolution with a product roadmap.
